About Us

In order to prevent your school from suffering financial losses as a result of debts, we help to facilitate and improve the process of recovering debt from defaulting students. In addition to giving you the chance to learn useful details about other community schools, our platform assists you in successfully recovering payments from defaulting students. Debtors are made accountable with the aid of Deudoras, which relieves you of your concerns.

Our Story

According to research, 30 percent of students have not yet paid for the previous year's tuition, which is indicative of the epidemic of parents' and guardians' irregular fee payments. Additionally, parents who transfer their kids to different schools without paying the necessary fees are trying to outsmart the school. We started by addressing the basic problems of students not paying their due fees and transferring to other schools as well as providing a trustworthy method of fee debt tracking. As a result, most schools close their doors due to a lack of funds used to maintain the school structure, leaving school owners at an all-time low and occasionally running into debt while attempting to maintain the school.

In order to address the issue, we at Deudoras conducted the required research to better understand our target audience. After reviewing the results of the research, we went on to develop this platform. In order to secure the smooth operation of their schools, our purpose was to assist in relieving school owners of the persistent problem of school fee defaulters.
