How Deudoras Work

Follow these 3 easy steps to view or add debtor

STEP 1

Sign Up For Free

Completing our easy step by step sign up form
gives you access to the platform as a parent or
school admnistrator.

STEP 2

Upload School Database

To help us track down school debtors upload the school databse
and verify the list of debtors.

STEP 3

Completion

After uploading the school database and
verifying the list of debtors, you can
frequently update the database when you
confirm the debtors dont owe anymore.

Our Goals

At Deudoras, We aim to provide a seamless platform for school owners which enables the schools to track
down school debtors around your locality while
maintaining the privacy of parents and students
debtors and making sure you stay on top of your
account balance to prevent shut down of your school.
