THE FAQS

Have any questions? We're here to assist you.

What is Deudoras

Deudoras is a platform that
allows schools in Gbagada
locality to list directory of
people owing them- to
help them avoid going to
other schools.

How does it work?

Schools in Gbagada
locality register their
schools and list the
students with outstanding
debts so other schools can
be aware. Parents can also
contend.

How do I check my Status

You can check your debt
status by registering as a
user and stating the school
you attend/attended and
the school ID given to you,
your status would appear
on your dashboard.

Can't upload proof?

If you can't upload your
proof of payment, it is most
likely you are uploading a
document/picture above
5MB.

How do I clear my debts?

You can clear your debts by
paying them, then dispute
the debt on your dashboard
and upload your proof of
payment.

How long is the review?

Reviews can take as long as
3 days, but you can speed
up the process by visiting
said school with your
receiptslproof of payment.
