Bernard Citadel of Learning Produces The Best Student

written by Adline

Uploaded 2 weeks ago

A student of Zuri International College, Surulere, Lagos, Miss. BIMPE Port, has obtained the best result in the 2022 West African Secondary School Certificate Examination (W.A.S.S.C.E) in Lagos State, by scoring eight distinctions. The proprietor of the college, Dr. Sunbo Oniyangi, presented her an award for excellence for making the school proud.

At the award ceremony, held during the valedictory service for the 2021/22 session, the proprietress of the college, Mr Mobolaji Oni, paid glowing tributes to Helena for being the best among equals, urging the school’s current final year students to struggle to beat her record.

Mr Oni said the aspiration of the founding fathers of the school was to make the institution the centre of excellence and number one college in the country. He said the college could boast of many of its products who are now lawyers, doctors, professors, engineers, computer analysts, etcetera holding high positions in the country today. Some of the ex-students graced the occasion.

The proprietress announced that from the next academic session the college would be introducing entrepreneurial skills in its curriculum, to enable the college’s products be employers of labour after graduation.

ALso speaking at the occasion, a senior staff of the college, Mr Adebisi, said he had no doubt that the school’s graduands today would climb the ladder of outstanding academic success “we have witnessed in our former students within and outside Nigeria”.

The proprietor of the college, Dr. Sunbo Oniyangi, told the graduands: “It is time to soar high above the rest of the pack as eagles. Let your imagination soar higher above your environment, challenge and limitation”.
